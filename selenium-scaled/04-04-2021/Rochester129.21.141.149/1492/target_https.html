Asempa Yetsia Cultural Troupe

Asempa Yetsia Cultural troupe core's aim is to educate children and young adults, through the spirit of music and performance, and help them improve their life through perseverance and hard work.

AYCT artist are endowed with skills and talents in various aspects of entertainment. In addition to their skill and talent, they share unique characteristics such as: they all experienced significant hardships growing up by Ghanaian standards. Their life experiences are a guiding force to their determination to succeed, and are commitment to be role models to todayâ€™s youth.

Honoring this commitment, AYCT gives free performances at schools and orphanages throughout Ghana. This is a highly-valued tradition in African culture, and one of the many ways they give back to the community.

The company also holds classes and workshops for talented young artists in music and dance, gear them towards achieving their goals, a means of self-expression, and an improved quality of life for themselves and their families.
