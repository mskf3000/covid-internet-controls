Nothing inspires me quite like French style where attention to detail is everything, from the vase that is chosen to gift wrapping and ribbons.
Most of all I enjoy talking to people to find out what they expect from the bouquet, what kind of impression do they want to make with it, how romantic, cheerful, thankful, dramatic, understated, or neither do they want it to be.
I take pleasure in what I do because I enjoy making people happy and forming beautiful bouquets always brings instant gratification.

Many thanks to those who influence me in my work Eugene Backe, Paula Pryke, Cate Kellison.

  • How to order flowers

    1. Decide on the arrangement size – Small, Medium or Large.

    2. Select the arrangement price.

    3. Click the BUY button to add arrangement to the shopping cart.

    4. Proceed to check out.

    All our arrangements shown in a medium size.

    Our basic delivery charge is $15.00 anywhere within San Francisco. We do deliver the same day but at least a day in advance notice is always appreciated. If you would like to specify time for delivery, please expect additional charge of $5.00. We do deliver Saturdays and Sundays.

    We accept Visa and Master Card either on line or over the phone, but are always happy to see you at our store in Noe Valley.
