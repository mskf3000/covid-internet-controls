37 Senators Join Chairman Menendez in Calling on President Biden to Recognize Armenian Genocide

702

-- Bipartisan Letter Demands an End to U.S. Complicity in Turkey’s Genocide Denial

WASHINGTON, DC – A bipartisan group of 37 Senators joined Senate Foreign Relations Committee Chairman Robert Menendez (D-NJ) today in calling on President Biden to follow the lead of Congress in fully and formally recognizing the Armenian Genocide.

“President Biden – by virtue of his own strong Senate record and the bipartisan House and Senate resolutions he backed as a candidate – is powerfully positioned to reject Turkey’s gag-rule, locking in permanent U.S. government-wide condemnation and commemoration of the Armenian Genocide,” said ANCA Executive Director Aram Hamparia (more...)

Bi-Partisan Measure Launched with Support of Over 30 U.S. House Members; ANCA Continues Campaign to Rally Support for Speedy Passage

UPDATE: Over 100 U.S. Representatives Call on Biden Administration to Stand with Artsakh and Armenia

876

-- Bipartisan Letter - Signed by Legislators from 20+ U.S. States – Advances Pro-Armenian Policy Priorities

WASHINGTON, DC – Over one hundred Members of Congress – from more than twenty U.S. states – have called upon the Biden Administration to undertake concrete steps for Artsakh’s survival and Armenia’s security, in a bipartisan Armenian Caucus letter strongly supported by the ANCA.
