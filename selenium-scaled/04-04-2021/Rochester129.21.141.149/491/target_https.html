The Harms

Did you know that the harms caused by alcohol and other drugs in Australia account for:

  • 4,186+ deaths per year

  • $23.5b combined economic cost

For over 60 years, we have worked to inspire positive change and deliver evidence-based approaches to minimise alcohol and drug harm.

We do this because we are working towards an Australia where lives are unlimited by alcohol and drug harm.
