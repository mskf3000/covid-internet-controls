Our Vision

Children and youth grounded in the learning of their community’s historical and cultural experiences and engaged in critical reflection and self-expression, are thus able to become strong advocates of community issues and active agents in their community’s development
A Closer Look

ARCPA

Al-JANA/ARCPA works with communities that face marginalization in Lebanon in building on their strengths, and documenting and disseminating their empowering experiences and cultural contributions

Arij Palestine Nights for Music Beddawi

by AL-JANA | Jun 3, 2016 | Main Posts, News and Activities

AL-JANA is proud to present the eighth edition of Arij Palestine Nights for Music.   Between 26 and 28 May 2016, audiences from the Palestinian camp in Beddawi and its surrounding areas will be able to enjoy three nights of free open air concerts, bringing together accomplished local and regional artists at the Palestine Playground.   Charbel Rouhana will be launching the concert series on Thursday, May 26, followed by an Oriental Night with Nisreen Hmaydan on Friday, 27 May. Macadi Nahhas will be closing the series on Saturday, 28 May.   Arij Palestine Nights for Music allows audiences to experience accomplished musicians live on a mobile stage and to welcome them in the heart of their neighborhood.
