Dean, Ayub Medical College

Prof. Dr Umer Farooq

Dr. Umer Farooq, Professor of Community Medicine has been appointed as Dean Ayub Medical College on October 12, 2018. He is an academician and a researcher with vast administrative experience. He has served in various administrative capacities and more recently held the post of Associate Dean Research.
