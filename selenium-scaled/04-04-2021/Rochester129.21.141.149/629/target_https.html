Welcome to Aide et Action International



Citizens of the world, join forces with our international movement to promote access to education for all through cooperation, empowerment and the development of skills of local populations. Aide et Action develops its action in a perspective of lifelong education, focusing its interventions on programmes and projects favouring the following, for the most marginalised and vulnerable populations:

  • Access and quality of Education at the primary and secondary level

    Promoting access to education for all (children, youth or adults) and improving educational systems to adapt them to the needs of all, so as to enable them to fully play their role as citizens.

  • Early childhood care and education

    Supporting the future development of children in the 0-6 year age group, including their schooling and in the physical, psychological, social and cognitive areas is fundamental for Aide et Action International.

  • Lifelong and Livelihood Education – Vocational training

    Creating educational alternatives to offer the most disadvantaged youth the opportunity to take their due place in society with dignity.
