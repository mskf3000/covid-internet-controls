About The Cover

ONLINE COVER Bridging Divides. This months cover highlights the 20th anniversary of the Federation of Clinical Immunology Societies (FOCIS), a Strategic Contributor to Science Immunology. The cover image shows the Verrazzano-Narrows Bridge in New York City. Excerpts of interviews with FOCIS cofounders David Hafler and Garry Fathman are featured in an Editorial that provides a behind-the-scenes look at the events that led to the formation of FOCIS and how the organization continues to pursue its mission of building bridges within the immunology community. [CREDIT: RAFAEL MACIA/SCIENCE SOURCE]
